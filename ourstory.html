Learn about Radxa

As a sub-brand of Radxa, Palmshell is committed to offering products with lower barriers to entry, making the benefits of technological progress accessible to everyone. Palmshell's relationship with Radxa is not one of separation but of integration. Leveraging Radxa's extensive experience in AIoT design and manufacturing, we are confident in our ability to deliver high-quality products to our users. Palmshell's product lineup encompasses audio-visual, entertainment, office, and home use. Compared to the open hardware platforms provided by Radxa, Palmshell aims to offer products that are more specialized in specific areas.

Bringing Technology to All!

We acknowledge that development boards and open-source hardware may not be suitable for everyone. We believe in advancing technology for the betterment of all humanity, valuing every user, and striving to create products that are more accessible to a wider audience.
